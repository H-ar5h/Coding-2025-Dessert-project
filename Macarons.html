Macarons

Macarons are delicate French sandwich cookies made from almond flour, egg whites, and sugar. These colorful treats consist of two smooth, dome-shaped shells with a creamy filling in between. The perfect macaron has a smooth top, ruffled "feet" at the base, and a chewy texture that gives way to the flavorful ganache, buttercream, or jam filling. Originally created in French monasteries, macarons have become a symbol of French patisserie excellence, available in countless flavors and colors that make them as beautiful as they are delicious.
