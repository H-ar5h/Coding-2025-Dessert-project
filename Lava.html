Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate Lava Cake, also known as molten chocolate cake, is a decadent dessert that delivers pure chocolate indulgence. This individual-sized cake features a firm chocolate exterior that gives way to a warm, flowing chocolate center when cut into. The "lava" effect is achieved by slightly underbaking the cake, leaving the center molten and gooey. Typically served warm with a dusting of powdered sugar and often accompanied by vanilla ice cream, this dessert creates a perfect contrast of temperatures and textures that chocolate lovers find irresistible.
