Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that combines layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers with a rich mascarpone cream mixture. This beloved dessert translates to "pick-me-up" in Italian, referring to its energizing coffee flavor. The traditional recipe includes eggs, sugar, mascarpone cheese, ladyfingers, coffee, and a dusting of cocoa powder. Its creamy texture and perfect balance of sweetness and coffee bitterness make it one of the most popular desserts worldwide.
